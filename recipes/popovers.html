Popovers

Description

Popovers are the American take on an English classic: the Yorkshire Pudding. This recipe is super easy and makes deliciously cripsy yet custardy in the middle popovers that are definitely going to go fast once you bake them!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Place popover tin in oven while it preheats.
  2. Beat eggs and milk together in a bowl. Add flour and salt. Beat until the batter is very smooth.
  3. Put 3/4 teaspoon butter in each popover tin and spread butter up sides of the cup. Quickly divide batter between the buttered cups.
  4. Bake 30-35 minutes or until popovers have risen and are brown. Do not open the oven while they bake.
